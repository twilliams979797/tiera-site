Who She Is

Tiera E. Williams is a solutions-oriented leader at the intersection of finance, education, and service. From the town of Cedar Lake, Texas to the Yale School of Management, she has never stopped pushing — or opening doors for those coming behind her.

As a Hechinger Scholar, MLT Fellow, and proud Howard University alumna, Tiera combines executive-level thinking with ground-level commitment to equity. A financial services strategist by training, a community builder by nature, and a crown-wearer by achievement.
